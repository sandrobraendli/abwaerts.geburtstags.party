A weekend with a hike, good food and a birthday party at a picturesque place

It turned out that Martin and Sandro will become old on almost the same day, which is July 2 for Martin and June 28 for Sandro. To us this seems like a good reason to organize something bigger together.

When?

June 24-25, 2017

While we recommend staying overnight, you can also join us just for the hike and/or the party on Saturday.

Those coming from further away, please let us know if you need help finding a couch in the region on Friday night.

Where?

Ferienheim St. Michael, 1716 Schwarzsee

Located just above the lake, close to "Schwarzsee, Bad" bus stop. Limited parking available.

The house provides 10 bedrooms with 1-3 beds each, 2 rooms with 4 beds (bunk beds) each, 2 separate dormitories sleeping 10 to 12 people each. Dinner and breakfast as well as drinks will be organized (wine, beer and more).

In total there is space for about 50 persons.

There will be a donation box, you can pay as much as you like. We estimate that we will spend around 30 CHF per person on food and accommodation.
