It tourned out that Martin and Sandro will become old at almost the same day, which is July 2 for Martin and June 28 for Sandro. To us this seems like a good reason to organize something bigger together.

Where

Naturfreundehaus Brambrüesch, 7074 Malix

The hut has two common rooms and several dorms with 4 to 10 beds. It sleeps 42 people in total. No bedsheets are provided, so please bring your sleeping bag. Dinner and breakfast as well as some drinks will be organized (a few bottles of wine but no beer).

You can get to the hut with the cable car from Chur. It is a 15-minute walk from Brambrüesch station. It can also be reached by car via Malix, although limited parking is available, so please let us know if you need a space. Of course one can also hike up to the hut from Chur, Malix, or Känzeli (the cable car's intermediate station). The hut is even visible on Google Street View!
